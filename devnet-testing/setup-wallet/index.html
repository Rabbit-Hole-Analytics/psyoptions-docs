Claiming Devnet Tokens - Walkthrough#

For PSY tokens, please go through the same method, and refer to the above mint and faucet address.

  1. Go to Sollet.io

  2. On the top right of Sollet, click "Mainnet Beta" and switch to "http://devnet.solana.com".

  1. Click "Request Airdrop" and wait for 1 SOL to be deposited into your wallet.

  2. Click on the "+" symbol to add a new token

  3. Select "Manual Input" and fill in the following information:

    1. Token Mint Address: HinfVnJuzMtJsyuLE2ArYCChDZB6FCxEu2p3CQeMcDiF
    2. Token Name: USDC
    3. Token Symbol: USDC

  4. Click add, refresh the wallet and wait for the address to load.

  5. Copy the Associated Token Metadata.

  6. Go to https://spl-token-ui.netlify.app/#/token-faucets

  7. In the top right, change to "devnet"

  8. Click "Token Airdrop"

  9. Fill in the following:

    1. Admin: Leave empty.
    2. Token Destination Address: Paste the copied Associated Token Metadata.
    3. Faucet Address: BmaVN3Wut1k2MtrcsxjTn919GdBJ2gzVLTtbiybnAPnR
    4. Amount: 1000000000000

  1. Click "Airdrop Tokens" and a Sollet Wallet window will pop-up.

  2. Switch to the Sollet Wallet window and click "Connect".

    1. Approve the transaction.

  3. Check your Sollet if you have received the airdropped tokens.
